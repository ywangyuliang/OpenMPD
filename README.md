# OpenMPD

OpenMPD is a research project from DATSI at UPM, developed under a collaboration grant from the Spanish Ministry of Education.

Authors: Yuliang Wang and Caijie Wu  
Supervisor: Antonio García Dopico

## Repository branches

- `main`: main branch of the repository.
- `release-final`: final version delivered to the supervisor and the public, with a complete refactor and improvement of the project.
- `version-after-fix-base-translator`: version of the base translator after reviewing and fixing previous errors. *(branch created during the TFG - Bachelor's/Master's thesis)*
- `yuliang-wang-task`: branch for the development of task support. *(branch created during the TFG)*
- `yuliang-wang-task-profiling`: branch for testing and profiling results of task support. *(branch created during the TFG)*
- `caijie-wu-halo`: branch for development related to halo. *(branch created during the TFG)*

For the latest version and documentation, see the [`release-final`](https://github.com/ywangyuliang/OpenMPD/tree/release-final) branch.

# OpenMPD

OpenMPD es un proyecto de investigación del DATSI de la UPM, desarrollado bajo la beca de colaboración del Ministerio de Educación.

Autores: Yuliang Wang y Caijie Wu  
Tutor: Antonio García Dopico

## Ramas del repositorio

- `main`: rama principal del repositorio.
- `release-final`: versión final entregable al profesor y al público, con refactor y mejora completa del proyecto.
- `version-after-fix-base-translator`: versión del traductor base tras revisar y corregir errores previos. *(rama creada durante el desarrollo del TFG)*
- `yuliang-wang-task`: rama del desarrollo del soporte de tareas. *(rama creada durante el desarrollo del TFG)*
- `yuliang-wang-task-profiling`: rama de pruebas y resultados de profiling del soporte de tareas. *(rama creada durante el desarrollo del TFG)*
- `caijie-wu-halo`: rama del desarrollo relacionado con halo. *(rama creada durante el desarrollo del TFG)*

Para la versión más reciente y su documentación, consulta la rama [`release-final`](https://github.com/ywangyuliang/OpenMPD/tree/release-final).
